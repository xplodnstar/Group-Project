• Our History

    Print of Ale was established in April of 2019. Originally conceived as a class project for their coding class, founders Tissiana and Nova came together with the idea to create an outlandish and fun concept that would please the almighty graders. The concept behind the tavern is to 3D print all of our food to make it as fresh as possible, but also to be located on Mars, just to differentiate us from all others. Tissi and Nova worked tirelessly to complete the website that would be considered the best in the class. Well, at least the most interesting anyway.
